Privacy Policy

Haruspis collects only what is needed to improve the Halo Wars mod experience. Here is the full breakdown.

At a glance

Browsing and installing mods does not require an account. Creator sign-in is handled with Discord OAuth for publishing tools only.

Data collection details

Transparent, minimal, and built for GDPR-friendly compliance.

Anonymous usage data

Haruspis collects anonymous usage data to help improve the app. We record the number of times a mod is clicked or launched to understand feature usage and highlight popular content. No personally identifying information is stored with these counts.

Discord OAuth for creators

Authentication uses Discord OAuth. When you sign in, we receive your Discord account ID and basic profile details required to manage your creator profile and mod ownership. We do not store your Discord password. Sign-in is only required for posting mods in the Creator tab.

No login for browsing

Browsing, installing, and launching mods never require a login.
